Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Xenotune collects, uses, and protects your information.

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Xenotune app created by Xenotrix as a Freemium service. It is provided "AS IS" without warranties of any kind, either express or implied, including but not limited to the implied warranties of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects usage data to improve user experience and application functionality. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • The specific pages visited within the application
  • Timestamps of visits and interactions
  • The duration of your session
  • Details about the operating system and device type used

We do not collect location-based information or personal identifiers unless explicitly provided by the user during account creation for premium services. Google Sign-In is optional and only required for premium transactions. No other data from your Google account is accessed or stored.

Third Party Access

To provide essential services, the Application incorporates third-party software libraries. These services may collect and use information under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Handles basic app functionality and updates.
  • AdMob: Displays in-app advertisements to support the freemium model.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Provides usage metrics, crash reports, and engagement analysis.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app purchases and subscriptions securely.

We only share data with these services as necessary for their intended purpose. No third party has permission to use your data independently outside the agreed terms.

Opt-Out Rights

You can easily stop all information collection by uninstalling the application. This can be done via your device’s standard uninstall process. You are encouraged to review your privacy settings regularly and disable any tracking features that you do not wish to be active.

Data Retention

Data collected through the app is retained as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services. Upon request, we will delete user data stored in our systems. To initiate deletion, please use the contact information found in the About section of the application.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly solicit or collect data from individuals under this age group. If we become aware that a child has submitted personal data, it will be immediately removed. Parents or guardians who suspect unauthorized data collection should contact us promptly.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. This includes the use of encrypted communications, access control systems, and routine security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page and indicated with a new effective date. Users are advised to review the policy regularly.

Consent

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact the Service Provider using the links provided in the About section of the app. We will respond promptly and resolve any concerns to the best of our ability.
